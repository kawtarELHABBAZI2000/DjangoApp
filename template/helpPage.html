Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I upload a medical scan?

To upload a medical scan, go to the main page of the Medical Scanner Prediction application and select the appropriate scan type (e.g., brain scan, lung scan, eye scan). Then click on the "Upload" button and follow the instructions to choose and upload your scan image file.

2. How long does it take to process a scan and get the prediction results?

The processing time may vary depending on the complexity of the scan and the server load. Generally, it takes a few seconds to a few minutes to process a scan and obtain the prediction results.

3. Are the prediction results accurate?

Our advanced models have been trained on a large dataset and have shown high accuracy in predicting various medical conditions. However, it's important to note that the predictions are not 100% definitive and should be used as a tool to assist medical professionals in making informed decisions.

4. Can I use this application for self-diagnosis?

No, the Medical Scanner Prediction application is not intended for self-diagnosis. It is designed to assist medical professionals in the diagnostic process and provide additional insights. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider for accurate diagnosis and treatment.
